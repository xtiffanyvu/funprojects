The Little
Oliva

215 Avenue I · Redondo Beach

A charming Mediterranean-Italian hidden gem steps from the beach. The Taormina pasta with sausage is a standout, truffle dishes are on the menu, and the lamb kabob with garlic-forward sides never disappoints. The cocktail list is fun and the patio is lovely. Closes at 9pm — aim to be seated by 7:45.
